Overall Assessment

The codebase has a gorgeous editorial character with slow, premium staggered animations. However, the transitions are incomplete: key items appear with delays but snap out instantly without exit animations, and tab interfaces swap immediately with zero visual continuity.

What's off

The tab switches (2) are instant swaps with no transition, creating a stark visual snap. In contrast, the general page elements take a long time to materialize, meaning the active navbar doesn't enter or exit cleanly when navigation swaps.

LiquidNavbar visibility toggle lacks exit animation

WhatWhen switching to pages that hide the navbar (Vortex or Soccer Team), the navbar unmounts instantly from the DOM. When returning to other pages, it plays a delayed slide down. No AnimatePresence wraps the render conditional.

Why it mattersThe visual contrast between a smooth entry and a sudden, abrupt exit makes the navigation transition feel unpolished and broken.

Recommended motionWrap the conditional render block in AnimatePresence, and add a matching exit slide-up + fade to the navbar container.

ImportantEmil

Font-weight switch on LiquidNavbar causes layout shift

WhatWhen hovering or selecting a tab in `LiquidNavbar`, the active state toggles class names from normal text to `font-bold` instantly. Because bold text is wider, this shifts the tabs to the right/left.

Why it mattersLayout shifts on hover or select feel jarring and cause micro-distractions. Affordance transitions should be visually stable.

Recommended motionKeep font-weight identical between states, and use a spacing adjustment, text-shadow, or color/underline transitions to mark active tabs without shifting layout.
