# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Creative directors, recruiters, other developers, and design-minded visitors. They are exploring the site to evaluate Cherif's technical craft, design aesthetics, and attention to interactive detail.

## Product Purpose
A high-fidelity, premium personal website, creative showcase, and interactive engineering sandbox demonstrating fluid web mechanics, physics-based motion, and bespoke user interfaces. Success is a digital experience that feels premium, tactile, and technologically extraordinary.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, Creative, Premium. Confident and refined, evoking a sense of meticulous craftsmanship, clean layout, and tactile physical feedback.

## Anti-references
Avoid SaaS clichés:
- No generic icon-plus-heading-plus-text card grids repeated endlessly.
- No neon purple/blue glassmorphism as decorative styling.
- No sketchy, hand-drawn or amateurish SVG illustrations.
- No big number hero metrics with tiny labels.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Prove engineering depth through custom interactive components, physics-based motion, and flawless visual execution.
- **Atmospheric Restraint**: Let negative space, structured typography, and harmonious color composition create impact, rather than decorative noise.
- **Tactile Kinematics**: Ensure every interaction (drag, scroll, hover) responds with fluid, organic motion and physics-based feedback.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast body text (minimum 4.5:1 ratio for readability).
- Dedicated support for `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
- Semantic HTML structure for screen readers and keyboard navigation.
